Where to start choosing accessories?  Yes, to decide what exactly to choose - a purse or a wallet? The handbag is exclusively an attribute of the male sex, while the wallet is an accessory for both women and men.

The men's wallet, which is similar in functionality to a woman's, has a stricter design, clean lines and the absence of decorative elements. It is a flat, horizontal or vertical type, with several compartments for notes and cards and (often) a hidden zip pocket.

The handbag is smaller than a wallet and folds two or three times. The buckle is missing or available in the form of a "strap" on the button.

A woman's wallet is a real space for design thinking. The women's wallet is always a large size and horizontal type, with many compartments inside and a closing button or zipper that runs all over the side. However, this is only a brief description and then we analyze in detail the properties of each accessory.

It is worth noting that today people call a wallet a wallet and vice versa (as they are used to or more comfortable), they do not know and do not think whether the definition is correct and whether there is any difference at all.

When choosing a wallet, the wallet is governed by five criteria - it is functionality, design, type of material, appearance and price of accessories.

          The following men's wallets are differentiated by size:


  • Breast Wallet - We also call them men's wallets, bills. Large vertical wallets with several internal compartments for banknotes and plastic cards. These are vertical wallets where banknotes are stored in full size without being folded. Such a flat wallet is comfortably carried in a breast pocket, in a compact bag and simply in the hand. Not suitable for carrying pants or jeans in your pocket due to its dimensions. There is usually no small pocket in the wallet for the chest pocket, but there is space for a driver's license (modern type, plastic) with a transparent window.
  • Bi-fold wallet - Then every known and most common type of wallet, or rather a wallet. It folds in half and is fastened either on a strap or simply, it bangs like a book. The men's double folded wallet has several compartments for banknotes, which are also folded in half. Along with the money compartments, there are pockets for plastic cards and often a coin compartment. Wallets vary in size and the smaller ones are great to wear in the back and front pockets of jeans, pants, jackets.
  • Tri-fold wallet - „Trifold“ is the most multifunctional type of wallet. It consists of three folding parts and has a large number of compartments and pockets, it all fits. „Triphold“ it is small in size, but thicker and bulkier than the "bifold" due to the three folded parts. Numerous branches allow you to take several plastic cards, credit cards, discount cards, season tickets, driver's licenses and banknotes of various currencies. Men's triple handbag, although stronger, fits perfectly in the pockets of jackets, jackets, purses / handbags.
